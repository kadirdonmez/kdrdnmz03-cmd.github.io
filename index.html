Profile & Research
Research Focus

What I Work On

My research develops optimization and data-driven decision support tools for real-world aviation operations — from runway scheduling under uncertainty to airport ground conflict detection using ADS-B surveillance data.

Core methodologies include multi-objective stochastic programming, matheuristic algorithms, deep learning for predictive maintenance, and human factors analysis (HFACS). I aim to bridge the gap between theoretical models and operationally deployable solutions for air traffic controllers and airport managers.
